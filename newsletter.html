How To Use Remarkable Marketingü§©

‚Ää

Remarkable marketing is the art of creating things worth noticing right into your product or service. It separates your offers from the noise and makes people pay attention.
 

To make your products as successful as possible, you need to offer things that your audience will find worth talking about. One of the best ways to do this is by having a remarkable slogan, fact, or policy that comes along with your product or service that is worth spreading the word about.

Another way is to offer something highly niche and unique. Here are some highly successful Stan Creators who offer remarkable products or special policies you can't find everywhere:


1. Luis Anthony Diaz (@Dispatchenespanol) offers a dispatcher's manual that teaches you about the transportation industry and how to work with trucks from your home. A highly unique and under-appreciated work-from-home side hustle.
 
2. Justine Treadwell (@thebalancedmethod) has created more products than nearly any other Stan user. Her strength, cardio, and flexibility packages come in different levels, so customers clearly understand the next purchase to continue to improve.
 
3. Jake McNeil (@jakecreativehackers) provides a single product. It is coaching to develop a personal brand for multipotentialities. He provides an actionable path to define who you are and what you do. To make his offer more remarkable, after the session, he will refund the entire amount if you don't feel you've received enough value.
